Who are we

We use the best tools of technology to help grow your business by creating powerful projects.

Albanian Business Partner sh.p.k. it's located in Tirana, with 10 years of experience in ICT consultancy services. We provide high-quality software development, software testing, team augmentation, web/app applications, and digital marketing. ABP is certified by TUV Austria, Information Technology – Service Management ISO/IEC 20000: 2011, and Information Security Management System ISO/IEC 27001: 2013. Our company is a member of the Albanian ICT Association, Albanian Consulting Network and Albanian Alliance for Apprenticeship.

We have successfully developed services of different scopes, in areas of health care, education, e-commerce, and other industries as well. Our goal is to provide high-efficiency projects by our talented teams using the best tools of technology. Constant investment in our people leads us to deliver qualitative services so our clients may achieve their expectations.

For years now, we work with partners such as Astraia Software GmbH, Nexus/AG, and many more that we keep building trust by time. We have and continue to deliver our ICT consulting services to private, public and non-governmental sectors.

We don’t just make deals! But, we build honest and transparent relationships based on continuous communication. This is how we create partnerships.

We are all part of the same team and care for their projects as much as they do!
